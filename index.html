Lesson 1: Greetings

Learn how to greet others in English. Greetings are essential in daily conversations.

  • Hello – Hola
  • Good morning – Buenos días
  • Good afternoon – Buenas tardes
  • Good evening – Buenas noches (al llegar)
  • Good night – Buenas noches (al despedirse)
  • How are you? – ¿Cómo estás?
  • I'm fine, thank you. – Estoy bien, gracias.
  • What's your name? – ¿Cómo te llamas?
  • My name is... – Me llamo...
  • Nice to meet you. – Mucho gusto.

Practice these greetings with friends and family to become more comfortable using them.
